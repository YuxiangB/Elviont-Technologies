AleaLens Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

AleaLens is an iOS app for organizing public market-research signals. The app does not require an AleaLens account and does not send personal profile data, contacts, location, photos, microphone, camera, or address book data to a developer server.

Information Stored On Your Device

AleaLens stores a small amount of preference state on your device, including language selection, notification baseline state, and public post IDs that have already triggered a local notification. This information is stored with iOS UserDefaults, is not used for cross-app tracking, and is not sold to third parties.

Network Requests

The app makes network requests to public data sources, including GitHub Raw, Yahoo Finance, Nasdaq, Stooq, and X links that you choose to open. Those third-party services may receive standard request information, such as IP address and device or browser request headers, and process it under their own privacy policies.

Subscriptions And Payments

AleaLens Pro is an auto-renewable subscription. Subscriptions, billing, refunds, trial eligibility, and payment information are handled by Apple. AleaLens does not receive your card number or Apple ID password.

Tracking And Advertising

AleaLens does not include advertising SDKs, does not use App Tracking Transparency tracking, does not sell personal data, and does not combine in-app data with other companies' data for targeted advertising.

Support

For content-rights, privacy, or support requests, contact the developer using the support contact listed on the App Store product page or visit the AleaLens support page.
