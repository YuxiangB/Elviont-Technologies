Privacy Policy

This policy explains what BetIntel uses, stores, and shares when you use the app.

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Overview

BetIntel helps users review football fixtures, match probabilities, AI answers, and manually entered bet records. BetIntel is an informational tool only. The app does not accept bets, deposits, withdrawals, or wagers.

We design BetIntel to use the least amount of personal information needed to provide the app experience.

Information We Use

  • Public match data: fixtures, scores, teams, competitions, venues, rankings, and match statistics from public or licensed sports data sources.
  • Manual bet records: bet selections, stake, odds, status, and related notes that you choose to enter in the app.
  • AI questions: questions you ask about a match and relevant match context needed to generate a response.
  • Subscription status: whether your Apple subscription is active, expired, restored, or unavailable.
  • App preferences and cache: local settings, recently loaded fixtures, and prediction refresh metadata used to make the app faster and more consistent.

Local Storage

Bet records, preferences, cached match information, and prediction metadata may be stored locally on your device. Removing the app may remove local data unless iOS or your device backup preserves it.

AI Processing

When you ask BetIntel AI a match-related question, the app may process the request using on-device Apple capabilities when available. If on-device processing is unavailable or cannot answer the request, the question and relevant match context may be sent to an AI provider to generate a short answer.

Do not include sensitive personal information in AI questions. AI responses are informational and should not be treated as financial, legal, or betting advice.

Purchases And Subscriptions

Subscriptions and payments are handled by Apple through the App Store. BetIntel does not receive or store your full payment card details. Apple may provide subscription status information so the app can unlock paid features, restore access, and manage introductory offer eligibility.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising tracking. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the app, process AI requests, provide subscription access through Apple, comply with law, or protect the app and users.

Retention And Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete records, clear app data, or remove the app. To request help with privacy questions or data concerns, contact support using the address below.

Children

BetIntel is not intended for children. The app is designed for users who are legally allowed to access sports analytics and betting-related information in their location.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through Apple on the BetIntel App Store listing.
